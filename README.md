# 🏫 BUNKR — Smart Attendance Tracker

The smart attendance tracker built for engineers who know when to grind — and when to bunk. Keep track of your college attendance seamlessly without the fear of falling under the 75% threshold.

🌐 **Live Site:** https://om-singh666.github.io/bunkr/

## ✨ Features

- **Real-time Calculations** — See how your attendance percentage changes instantly as you mark classes present or absent.
- **Day-wise Planner** — Set up your Monday-Friday academic schedule in seconds.
- **75% Rule Guardian** — Visual indicators (green, yellow, red) that constantly monitor your attendance so you never accidentally slip below the required threshold.
- **Beautiful UI** — Dark theme with a refined glassmorphism dashboard, glowing particle backgrounds, and dynamic animations.
- **Local Storage Persistence** — Your data lives directly on your device. Zero loading screens.

## 🤔 Why I Developed This

As an engineering student, managing attendance is a strategic game. You need to know exactly how many classes you can afford to miss to work on actual skills, projects, or just take a break — without failing the semester due to low attendance. Existing university portals are slow, clunky, and don't tell you the "what if" scenarios. 

**BUNKR** was built to solve this. It gives you immediate transparency into your attendance status. Put in your current stats, set up your week, and let BUNKR do the math so you can focus on what actually matters.

## 🚀 Built With

- **React / Vite** for lighting fast performance
- **OGL / WebGL** for the interactive particle background
- **Vanilla CSS** for the custom styling and glassmorphism effects
- **Lucide Icons**

## 💻 How to Use

1. Enter your current Total Classes Conducted and Classes Attended.
2. Setup your timetable by adding your daily subjects.
3. Every day, simply use the dashboard to mark whether you attended or bunked a class.
4. Watch the meters update automatically — green means you're safe, yellow means be careful, red means danger zone!
